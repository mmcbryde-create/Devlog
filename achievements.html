Capstone United Airlines Project

I served as a Business/Data Analyst, and developed a winning prototype for a centralized employee insights application designed to aggregate customer feedback from multiple platforms. The goal of the project was to address fragmented feedback systems by creating a single, streamlined interface that delivers actionable insights to improve customers end-to-end experience. Our solution demonstrated a clear understanding of United's RFP requirements, aligned with their strategic goals, and successfully translated complex, multi-source data into an accessible and scalable front-end prototype. The project was selected as the winning solution based on clarity, accuracy, creativity, and business impact.#TEAMMETIS
